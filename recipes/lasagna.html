Lasagna

Description

This is an italian baked pasta dish!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructons
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
