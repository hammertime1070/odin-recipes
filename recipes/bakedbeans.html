Baked Beans

Description

This is a Boston Baked Beans Recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups dry navy beans
  • 1/2 pound uncooked bacon strips/li>
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 3 tablespoons molasses
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 taplespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dry mustard

Steps

  1. Transfer beans and soaking water to a saucepan
  2. Bring to a boil and simmer until tender
  3. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F
  4. Arrange 1/2 of the beans in the bottom of a casserole dish. Place 1/2 of the bacon srips over the beans and 1/2 of the onions over top. Repeat the layers.
  5. Combine ketchup, molasses, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper and mustard. Bring to a boil
  6. Pour sauce over the beans
  7. Bake in the oven for 1 1/2 hours.
