Dump Cake

Description

This is a sick cake recipes!

Ingredients

  • 1 package yellow cake mix
  • 1 package instant chocolate pudding mix/li>
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 2/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 contianer sour cream
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a Bundt pan.
  2. Mix all ingredients and pour into the Bundt Pan
  3. Bake in the oven for 55 minutes. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before transferring to cooling racks.
